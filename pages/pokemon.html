Overview

Pokemon: Ancient Past and Bright Future is a Pokemon design prompt created by me as a challenge to create a wholly original Pokemon game that takes place in a region based on Canada. The final concept ended up being the 'Anata Region'. I decided on a theme of the past vs the future, or progress vs tradition (before Pokemon: Scarlet and Violet tackled very similar themes). I had many aspects planned out, such as characters, gym leaders, towns, story beats, mechanics, and most importantly, the Pokemon. I wanted to create a region of wholly new and original Pokemon ideas. This is a project I tend to come back to often after taking breaks to work on other things. One day I hope to make it into a fully-fledged Pokemon fan game.

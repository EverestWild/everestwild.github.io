You wake up in a mysterious world full of magic and monsters, with no recollection of how you got there! Get a job at the local tavern as you learn the mysteries of this world and meet its inhabitants. Use your free time between shifts to explore the world and spend quality time with your friends, opening up potential avenues for mystery, adventure, and maybe even romance if you choose your words wisely! Will you adjust to life in the new world? Or work tirelessly to find a way home?

You're here for a reason, but what is that reason, exactly? It's up to you to find out!

Wildwood is a story-driven visual novel with an emphasis on fantasy, steamy romance, adventure, and comedy. In Wildwood, your bonds are your strongest asset, and it's important to make the right choices and build lasting bonds with the people you meet. Some bonds may even be able to develop into full-fledged relationships!

Diverse Cast

The game boasts a large cast of heroes, villains, humans, and non-humans all designed, created, and written by me! Each character has their own plot line that develops throughout the main story, with many arcs crossing over and intertwining with each other as the plot thickens!

Bond System

Throughout the game, the player can develop bonds with the varying cast of characters. Some can even develop into fully fledged relationships! Be sure to make the right choices as they can have an effect on how certain characters view you, every choice is remembered!

Custom UI

Early in the game, the main character receives a cell phone. This cell phone tracks player progress, player relationships, and even has a social media where the cast periodically post photos and updates!

Custom Maps

Every map in the game has been custom-made by me to exactly fit the game's scenes. This includes an entire inn and tavern, a fancy mansion, a bustling town square, a forest clearing, and more!

About Me

A jack of all trades and a creative at heart, I am a dedicated video game developer and multimedia creator with over 8 years of experience in design, programming, art, audio, graphics, writing, and modelling. After getting a few years of professional game development experience, I have recently made a return to freelance work. Over the years I have involved myself in many gaming communities and fan projects under the moniker 'ShadOblivion', including the Pokemon, Super Smash Bros., Danganronpa, and Team Fortress 2 communities.

I'm a graduate from the University of Calgary with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a concentration in computer game design. I'm drawn to all sorts of activities and projects, but I thrive in environments where I can stretch my imaginative muscles and design something fun. Be it levels, weapons, or mechanics, this is where I am most in my element.

On my Instagram account, I post my own art relating to the video games I am interested in and personal projects that I'm currently working on. On YouTube, I am part of a channel called SoiBois, where me and my friends play video games and commentate.

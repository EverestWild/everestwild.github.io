About Me

I'm a graduate from the University of Calgary with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a concentration in computer game design. I'm drawn to all sorts of activities and projects, but I thrive in environments where I can stretch my imaginative muscles and design something fun. Be it levels, weapons, or mechanics, this is where I'm most in my element.

Online, I am known under my alias ShadOblivion. On my Instagram account, I post my own art relating to the video games I am interested in. On Youtube, I am part of a channel called SoiBois, where me and my friends play video games and commentate. I have also made myself known in a few internet communities, including TF2Maps, where I have been creating and prototyping maps for the online multiplayer game Team Fortress 2.
